DEVELOPER APPLICATION

Interested in becoming a developer for MoonScripts?

We're looking for developers who can create quality scripts, help community members, and contribute to the growth of the server.

Before applying, make sure you have:

  • Some scripting experience
  • Past work or projects to show
  • A good attitude and willingness to help others
  • The ability to work with other developers

To apply, join our Discord server and open a ticket or contact staff with examples of your previous work.
